About KLU

KLU integrates with 3D part catalogs, understands connections, and generates validated assemblies using AI. We connect to CAD and FEA tools for validation and support direct-to-delivery workflows.

Our vision is to make advanced design accessible to everyone—from engineers to imaginative kids—unlocking a new era of custom manufacturing and product innovation.

Key Features

  • AI-driven connection logic for practical assembly design
  • Integration with existing CAD, FEA, and inventory systems
  • Custom constraints for company-specific needs
  • Supports industries from toys (e.g., LEGO) to industrial suppliers (e.g., McMaster-Carr)
  • Direct-to-delivery pipeline with part sourcing and packaging APIs
